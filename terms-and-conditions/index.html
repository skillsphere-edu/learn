Section 07

Cloudflare KV Certificate Infrastructure

All issued Certificates are recorded in Cloudflare Workers KV, a globally-replicated key-value edge database. By accepting these Terms, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

  • Permanent storage: Your certificate record, identified by your unique Credential ID, will be stored in the KV Store indefinitely to ensure permanent verifiability. This is a core feature of the service, not incidental data retention.
  • Global distribution: KV data is replicated to multiple geographic regions globally. By accepting these Terms, you consent to this international transfer as necessary for service delivery.
  • Immutability: Certificate records in KV are designed to be immutable after issuance. Any modification (e.g., reissuance following a name correction) is logged in our audit system and involves retiring the prior Credential ID.
  • Public verification: Your Credential ID can be used to verify your Certificate at skillsphere-edu.github.io/verify.html?id=[credential-id]. This verification endpoint is public. Only certificate fields (not personal contact details) are returned.
  • Service continuity: SkillSphere makes reasonable efforts to maintain availability of the verification service. However, temporary unavailability due to Cloudflare infrastructure issues is outside our control and does not constitute a breach of these Terms.
Employer/Institutional verification: Third parties (employers, universities, professional bodies) may query the verification endpoint directly. SkillSphere Edu may provide bulk verification APIs to authorised institutional partners. If you do not wish your certificate to be publicly verifiable, please contact skillsphere.work@gmail.com — note that suppression renders the Certificate unverifiable.
