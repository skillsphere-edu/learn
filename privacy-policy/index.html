Section 01

Overview

SkillSphere Edu ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SkillSphere Edu platform — a suite of online learning tools, course catalogs, and verified certificate services accessible at skillsphere-edu.github.io/learn and associated domains and sub-domains (collectively the "Platform").

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use the Platform, why we collect it, how we store and process it, and what rights you hold. It should be read alongside our Terms & Conditions.

Plain-Language Summary We collect only what we need to deliver the service. Certificate records are permanently stored in Cloudflare's KV infrastructure so your credentials remain verifiable at all times. We never sell your personal data — ever.
Section 02

Data We Collect

We collect information in two ways: data you actively provide, and data gathered automatically as you interact with the Platform.

Information You Provide

  • Account Data — your full name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile Data — optional biography, profile photo, job title, and social links.
  • Payment Data — processed by Razorpay. We store only the last four digits of your card and a tokenised reference; full card numbers never touch our servers.
  • Course Activity — lesson completions, quiz scores, progress percentages, and assignments you submit.
  • Communications — messages sent via support, official social media handles, discussion forums, or community features.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device & Browser Data — IP address, browser type and version, operating system.
  • Usage Data — pages visited, time-on-page, click paths, referrer URLs, and video-play events.
  • Cookies & Local Storage — session tokens, preference flags, and analytics identifiers (see Section 09).

Section 03

Certificate Data

SkillSphere Edu issues verified digital certificates upon successful completion of eligible courses. The following information forms part of your certificate record and is treated with the highest level of data integrity:

  • Your full legal name as provided at registration
  • The title and unique identifier of the completed course
  • Date of issue and, where applicable, date of expiry
  • Unique Certificate Credential ID (UUID)
  • Final assessment score or grade
  • Issuing instructor name and SkillSphere digital signature
Public Verifiability Each certificate is publicly verifiable at skillsphere-edu.github.io/learn/verify.html?id=[credential-id]. Anyone with this URL can confirm authenticity. Only selective fields from the list above are exposed — no email address or payment data is ever visible publicly.

You may request removal of your certificate from the public verification page by contacting skillsphere.work@gmail.com. Doing so renders the certificate unverifiable, and SkillSphere Edu cannot be held responsible for any employment or accreditation implications that may follow.

Section 04

Cloudflare KV Storage & Infrastructure

SkillSphere Edu stores all certificate records in Cloudflare Workers KV — a globally-distributed, edge-replicated key-value data store operated by Cloudflare, Inc. We use Cloudflare KV for the following reasons:

  • Low-Latency Global Access — verification requests are served from the edge node closest to the verifier, minimising response time worldwide.
  • High Availability & Durability — KV data is replicated across Cloudflare's global network, ensuring certificates remain accessible even during regional outages.
  • Immutability by Design — certificate entries are write-once during normal operation. Modifications require explicit administrative approval and are fully audit-logged.

Data Stored in Cloudflare KV

Each KV entry is keyed by the Credential ID and contains only the certificate fields enumerated in Section 03. No passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal information are stored in KV.

Cloudflare as a Sub-Processor

Cloudflare, Inc. operates as a data processor/sub-processor for Indian customers, ensuring compliance with the Information Technology Act and SPDI Rules via a Data Processing Addendum (DPA). Data stored in KV may be cached across various geographic regions.

Data Location Primary KV writes are directed to Cloudflare's US and EU data centres. You may request information about specific regions holding your certificate data by emailing skillsphere.work@gmail.com.
Section 05

How We Use Your Data

We use personal data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery — creating and maintaining your account, tracking learning progress, and issuing certificates.
  • Payment Processing — completing transactions via Razorpay and managing refunds.
  • Certificate Issuance & Verification — writing, storing, and serving certificate records via Cloudflare KV.
  • Customer Support — responding to enquiries, resolving disputes, and debugging technical issues.
  • Platform Improvement — aggregated, anonymised analytics to understand how learners use the Platform.
  • Legal Compliance — meeting obligations under applicable law, including responses to lawful requests from regulators and courts.
  • Marketing Communications — only where you have given explicit opt-in consent; withdrawable at any time.
What We Do Not Do We do not use your data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects, nor do we build individual advertising profiles for any purpose whatsoever.
Section 06

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Cloudflare, Inc. — for KV storage and network infrastructure (see Section 04).
  • Razorpay — for secure payment processing.
  • Email Providers — for transactional emails, under a signed DPA.
  • Analytics Tools — only anonymised or aggregated data; no personally identifiable information is shared.
  • Legal Authorities — where required by law, court order, or to protect the rights of SkillSphere Edu and its users.
  • Business Transfer — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale; you will be notified in advance.

Section 08

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may hold some or all of the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — request correction of inaccurate data. Correcting your name on a certificate may require reissuance (paid service) and will be fully logged.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal retention obligations and the certificate retention policy above.
  • Restriction — request that we limit processing while a dispute is being resolved.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent — withdraw marketing consent at any time via account settings or by emailing us directly.

To exercise any right, contact skillsphere.work@gmail.com. We will acknowledge within 8–10 business days and resolve within 30 days.

Section 09

Cookies & Tracking

We use the following categories of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Strictly Necessary — session tokens and CSRF protection. These cannot be disabled.
  • Functional — language and display preferences.
  • Analytics — aggregated page-view data (privacy-preserving; no fingerprinting). Requires your consent.
  • Marketing — conversion pixels on marketing pages only. Requires your explicit consent.

Cookie preferences can be managed via the consent banner on first visit, or at any time through Account Settings → Privacy. Disabling analytics or marketing cookies will not affect access to courses or certificates.

Note on Our Current Hosting Our main website is currently hosted on GitHub Pages, a platform that does not store user information or place cookies. Our e-Learning Platform (separate service) may store user information strictly for the purposes defined in Section 05.
Section 10

Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
  • Role-based access controls limiting internal data access strictly on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular penetration testing and vulnerability scanning
  • Cloudflare DDoS mitigation and WAF protecting all API endpoints
  • Full audit logs for all administrative access to KV certificate records

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of it.

Section 11

Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended for users aged 18 and over. For users between the ages of 16 and 18, Guardian's Approval must be obtained before using the Platform. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we discover we have inadvertently done so, we will delete it promptly.

It is not the responsibility of SkillSphere Edu to seek or verify Guardian's Approval where required. It will be presumed that the user has already obtained the necessary permission. If you believe a child has provided us personal data without appropriate consent, contact skillsphere.work@gmail.com immediately.

Section 12

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email to all registered users, or by a prominent notice on the Platform and Official Social Media Handles, at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

Continued use of the Platform after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We recommend reviewing this page periodically to stay informed.
