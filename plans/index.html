Everything you need to know before signing up.

The certificate itself is identical — same design, same verification ID. The difference is cost and dispatch speed. On Starter you pay a small per-certificate fee and wait 2–3 days. On Pro it's included in your subscription with 36-hour dispatch. On Elite, dispatch happens within 24 hours.
