About SkillSphere Edu

Built by CA Students, For CA Students

We started SkillSphere Edu because we were frustrated. Frustrated by courses which taught theory without practice. Frustrated by certificates which the employers didn't trust. Frustrated by the gap between what books teach and what the industry actually needs.

So we built it ourselves — a platform run by CA students, shaped by real-world experience, and committed to one thing: helping the upcoming league of professionals get genuinely skilled and genuinely recognised for it.

"Education should be affordable, accessible, practical, and credible. SkillSphere Edu exists to make all four a reality for every CA student."
