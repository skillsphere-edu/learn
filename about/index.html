Case Log · Our Story

How This Case File Opened

Priyanshi, Naufil, and Mishthi were three CA students doing what CA students do — chasing exam deadlines, sitting through articleship hours, and studying subjects built for a syllabus, not for a job. Somewhere in that grind, all three noticed the same gap: the theory was rigorous, but nobody was teaching the practical, day-to-day skills that actually get used at a desk.

Certificates were part of the problem too. Plenty of platforms handed them out — almost none of them could be verified by an employer in under a minute. So the credential meant a lot to the learner and very little to anyone reading a resume.

In April 2026, instead of waiting for someone else to fix it, the three of them built SkillSphere Edu — a platform run by students, for students, where every certificate is checkable, every course is built around a real outcome, and the person answering your email actually knows what CA Inter feels like.

Case Background · The Complaint

What We're Filing Against

Every CA and commerce student in India runs into some version of the same five problems. We built SkillSphere Edu to address them directly.

01

Theory Without Practice

Years of coursework, almost none of it hands-on. Students graduate knowing the syllabus and not much about how the work actually gets done.

02

Unverifiable Certificates

A PDF with a signature isn't proof of anything. Employers have no fast way to confirm a certificate is real.

03

Scattered Opportunities

Internships and entry roles are posted everywhere and nowhere — buried in group chats, expired by the time they're found.

04

Generic, One-Size Content

Most upskilling platforms are built for a general audience. Almost none are built specifically around CA and commerce workflows.

05

No One Who's Been There

Support teams that have never sat a CA exam or handled a live GST filing don't know what a student is actually stuck on.
