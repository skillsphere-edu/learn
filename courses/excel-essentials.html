Case File · Overview
About This Course

Microsoft Excel remains one of the most-sought-after workplace skills across every industry in India — from CA firms and banks to startups and MNCs. Yet most students graduate without ever learning it properly. This course fixes that in five focused hours.

Every module is built around practical outcomes. You will not sit through theoretical slides — you will open Excel, apply what you just learned, and complete a real exercise before moving on. By the final lesson, you will have built a complete expense analysis dashboard from scratch.

The free verified certificate is included by default. It carries a unique ID that employers and firms can verify instantly at our public verification portal — no PDFs to question, no trust issues.

Who This Is For

  • →CA students and commerce graduates building industry-ready skills
  • →Freshers and students looking to strengthen their résumé before placement season
  • →Article trainees and interns who need to work with Excel data at firms
  • →Anyone who has avoided Excel and wants to finally learn it properly

Requirements

  • →No prior Excel experience needed — we begin from absolute zero
  • →A laptop or PC with Microsoft Excel (2016 or newer) or Excel Online
  • →Willingness to practice — not just watch. Each lesson has an exercise file
